Why Use a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) enhances your online privacy by masking your IP address and encrypting your internet traffic. It keeps your data safe from hackers and surveillance, especially on public Wi-Fi.

VPNs also allow access to geo-restricted content, ensure secure online transactions, and prevent tracking from advertisers and third-party apps.
